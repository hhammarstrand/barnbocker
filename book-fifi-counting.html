Fifi the Fox and Oliver the Owl Count in the Sunny Meadow
Look! One big red apple lies on the soft grass. Fifi and Oliver giggle with delight.
Two orange butterflies dance in the warm sunshine. 'Two!' shouts Fifi, clapping her tiny paws.
Three pink flowers sway gently beside a sparkling stream. 'Three!' says Oliver, his spectacles shining.
Four smooth grey pebbles sit in a sunny spot. 'Four!' chirps Fifi, picking one up.
Five golden dandelions flutter like little suns. 'Five!' they cheer, counting together with happy hearts.
